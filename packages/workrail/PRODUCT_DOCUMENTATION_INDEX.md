### Pricing Decision Framework

**When to Change Pricing**:
- Competitor moves
- Value delivery increases
- Market feedback
- Enterprise requirements
- Economic conditions

**When NOT to Change**:
- Short-term ARR pressure
- Single customer request
- Competitor's list price (check discounts)
