## Delivery Guidelines

### Vocal Pacing
- **Normal speed**: 140-160 words per minute
- **Slow down for**: Key concepts, core message, climax
- **Speed up for**: Lists, background info, transitions

### Energy Levels
- **High**: Opening, insight reveal, demo intro, climax, closing
- **Medium**: Journey, architecture, team impact
- **Measured**: Demo narration (let visuals lead)
- **Suspenseful**: Pre-climax build

### Emphasis Words/Phrases
- "Structure elevates creativity"
- "Reliable excellence" (not random)
- "Real work, unattended"
- "Not just better results—MORE results"
- "Certainty with evidence"

### Pauses (Critical for Impact)
- **Long pauses (3+ sec)**: Before climax reveal, after key questions
- **Medium pauses (1-2 sec)**: Between major points, let ideas land
- **Short pauses (<1 sec)**: Natural speaking rhythm

## Recording Tips

### Before Recording
- [ ] Warm up voice (hum, read aloud)
- [ ] Water nearby (room temperature)
- [ ] Script printed or on teleprompter
- [ ] Comfortable position
- [ ] Deep breath, relax shoulders

### During Recording
- Smile while speaking (conveys in voice)
- Maintain energy throughout
- If you mess up, pause and restart that sentence
- Don't worry about perfection - editing will fix it
- Trust the pauses - don't rush them

### After Recording
- Listen back immediately
- Note any sections that need re-recording
- Check timing against checkpoints
- Verify all key phrases are emphasized
- Ensure pauses are actually there
