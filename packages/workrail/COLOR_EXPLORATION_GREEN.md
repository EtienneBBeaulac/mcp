# Green Color Options

## Chrome's Green
**Colors:** Chrome uses multiple greens
- Main: `#1A73E8` (actually blue!) 
- Green icon: `#34A853` (Google Green)
**Vibe:** Calm, reliable, "go", success

## Quiet Green Variations

### Option A: Chrome/Google Green
**Color:** `#34A853` (Medium Green)
**Hex:** #34A853
**Why:** Proven by Google, familiar, trustworthy
**Used by:** Chrome, Android, Google ecosystem

### Option B: Emerald Green
**Color:** `#10B981` (Emerald 500)
**Hex:** #10B981
**Why:** Modern, vibrant but not loud, "success"
**Used by:** You already have this! (status-success)

### Option C: Teal Green
**Color:** `#14B8A6` (Teal 500)  
**Hex:** #14B8A6
**Why:** Green-blue hybrid, calm, modern
**Used by:** You have this too! (accent-teal)

### Option D: Sage Green (Very Quiet)
**Color:** `#22C55E` (Green 500)
**Hex:** #22C55E
**Why:** Softer than emerald, very calm
**Used by:** Health, wellness, calm apps

### Option E: Forest Green (Deep & Quiet)
**Color:** `#059669` (Green 600)
**Hex:** #059669
**Why:** Darker, more sophisticated, "quiet luxury"
**Used by:** Premium tools, John Deere, Land Rover

## Green Personality

**What Green Communicates:**
- Success, validation, "go"
- Growth, progress
- Calm, stable, natural
- Approachable, friendly
- "It's working"

**Pairs well with:**
- Amber/Yellow (for warnings/attention)
- Blues (for trust combo)
- Warm grays (natural feel)
- White (clean, fresh)

## Green vs Purple

**Green says:** "Reliable, working, successful, go"
**Purple says:** "Innovative, AI-focused, creative, future"

**Green is more:** Calm, stable, universal
**Purple is more:** Exciting, trendy, AI-specific
