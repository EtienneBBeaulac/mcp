## When Strokes Matter

### Scenarios where stroke helps:
1. **Logo on photos** - Complex/busy backgrounds
2. **User-generated content** - Can't control background
3. **Social media** - Various platform backgrounds
4. **Merchandise** - Printed on various materials/colors
5. **Very small sizes** - Extra definition helps legibility

### Scenarios where stroke doesn't matter:
1. **Our website** - We control backgrounds
2. **Documentation** - Clean, controlled
3. **Favicon** - Too small to see stroke anyway
4. **README** - Solid background colors
5. **Presentations** - We choose slide colors
